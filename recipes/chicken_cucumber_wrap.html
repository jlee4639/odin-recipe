Chicken Cucumer Wrap


Home

These green goddess chicken and cucumber wraps are deliciously fresh-tasting. Chicken, avocado, cucumber, and mozzarella, dressed with green goddess dressing, fill a spinach tortilla wrap, and then are tied with a green onion leaf or chive for a beautiful presentation. This recipe makes 2, but is very easy to double.


Ingredients

  • Spinach Tortillas
  • Leaf Lettuce
  • Mozzarella Slices
  • Cucumber
  • Avocadoes
  • Sweet Onion
  • Green Goddess Salad Dresing
  • Onion Leaves
  • Grape Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cover a tortilla with a piece of lettuce and top it with a mozzarella slice, shredded chicken, and cheese
  2. Cut cucumber in half and add to the tortilla pile
  3. Add half of the avocado and a table spoon of the Green Goddess Salad Dressing to the Tortilla
  4. Close the torilla with Green Onion leaf or Chive
